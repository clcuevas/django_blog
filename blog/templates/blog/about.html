About Me

In depth...

I am a first generation Mexican-American. I was born and raised in Southern California, but moved to Seattle 4 years ago because I enjoy the city's culture, coffee, geography, and weather.

In my free time, I enjoy playing or watching baseball. Although I support our Seattle Mariners team, my heart still belongs to the blue and red--Angels and Dodgers. I also enjoy hiking the Cascades and when time allows, I pack my day pack and hit the road to our National Forests. I am a strong believer in allowing yourself to unplug from your every day life and technology for a little R&R.
